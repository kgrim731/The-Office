The Office

Ashley Stec

 

Ashley "Ash" Stec (born August 9, 1993) is a fictional character on the U.S. television sitcom The Office, played by Ashley Stec. Her character is initially a receptionist at the paper distribution company Dunder Mifflin, until her termination after having a fall out with Dwight over what was actually false. Her character is very agressive and does not take shit from anyone including Dwight. She often gets in arguments with people and loves to pull pranks while no one is watching. She gained many fans throughout her run on the show but in the series finale she comes back and is married to a man with really nice arms.
