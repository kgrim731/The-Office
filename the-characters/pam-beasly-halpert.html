The Office

Pam-Beasly-Halpert

Pamela Morgan "Pam" Halpert née Beesley (also Beesley)[2] (born March 25, 1979) is a fictional character on the U.S. television sitcom The Office, played by Jenna Fischer. Her counterpart in the original UK series of The Office is Dawn Tinsley. Her character is initially the receptionist at the paper distribution company Dunder Mifflin, before becoming a saleswoman and eventually office administrator until her termination in the series finale. Her character is shy, growing assertive but amiable, and artistically inclined, and shares romantic interest with Jim Halpert, whom she begins dating in the fourth season and marries and starts a family with as the series continues. Pam's kindness and compassion means she is well liked among her workmates and develops strong relations with several of the office workers as the season progresses.
